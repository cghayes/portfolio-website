Birds [July 2020]

Birds are intertwined with human culture and our everyday experiences. They exist virtually wherever we go, from around the house to bustling cities to the remote wilderness. While it's common not to pay too much attention to the birds around us, taking a closer look at them is quite fascinating. Photographing birds is challenging and can be incredibly rewarding, especially when you capture that moment that is only seen for a fraction of a second by the naked eye.

Photo Submission and Voting

Submitting Photos

We supply three photo categories each month for you to submit photos and votes. You are allowed to submit one photo per category. Once you upload your photo, it is reviewed before being posted to the gallery. If your photo does not adhere to the aforementioned terms, it will not be approved and you will receive notification that it has not been posted.

Acceptable file formats are .jpg, .jpeg, and .png. There is a 2,000KB size limit.

New submissions are accepted until midnight (11:59 PM UTC) on the last day of the month.

Voting on Photos

You are allowed to submit one one vote per category. You may not vote for your own photo. Voting polls close at 11:59 PM UTC on the first day of the next month (e.g. voting for May submissions will end on June 1 at 11:59 PM).

Winning Photos

Photos that receive the most votes will be displayed, with credit to the photographer, in the winners gallery.

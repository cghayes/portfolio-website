Chat Room Conduct

The chat room is a friendly space available for Photoplex users to ask questions, share their thoughts and get advice. Be courteous by acknowledging new visitors when they enter the chat; we want everyone to feel welcome and supported. Feel free to share your thoughts respectfully and minimize chances of conflict by remaining open minded. We all have different opinions and experiences.

Basic Chat Room Guidelines:

  • Respect each other. Think before you write, ask yourself how would you feel if someone wrote this to you.
  • Offensive, rude or otherwise unwanted behavior may cause you to be reported/banned.
  • Help others. You are an important member of the community by sharing your knowledge.
  • Ask questions. Feel free to ask questions and strike up conversations with others.
  • Answer questions. If you see a question you know the answer to, feel free to reply & help out.
  • Encourage others. If a fellow photographer seems to be struggling, cheer them up!

FAQ

Can I return a disc after I've tried it out?

Yes! We offer a flexible return policy on our discs. Discs that have been used can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a store credit.

While our primary goal is to provide you with the most accurate information possible on discs before you purchase, we understand sometimes discs can behave a little differently from what you expected.

Please note: returned discs must be in like-new condition. If you think you may want to return a disc, we recommend testing it out in an open field. Discs must be returned clean and without any dings, scrapes, grass stains, or other damage. Any stickers that came on the disc must not be removed.

Please visit our Shipping & Returns page for more information on how to process a return.


How can I track my order?

When you first place your order, we will email you confirmation that your order has been processed. When your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number.

Please visit our Shipping & Returns page for more information on order progress and shipping.

Discs

We offer the best disc golf discs from top manufacturers. Every disc is provided with the most accurate details possible, including manufacturer specs and specific weights and colors. We weigh every disc!

Distance Drivers

Distance drivers have higher speeds and are meant to fly the farthest. They have the widest rims and lowest profiles.

Control Drivers

Control drivers offer great distance and allow you to maintain great control without having to be thrown as hard as distance drivers.

Midranges

Midranges are extremely versatile. They can be used for both drives and upshots and are generally easy to control.

Putters

Putt and approach discs are slow-flying and used for shorter tee shots and, of course, putting. It's imperative to have a good, reliable putter in your bag (or a few!).

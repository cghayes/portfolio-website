Description

The Paul McBeth Luna is one of the most popular Discraft discs on the market right now. Co-designed with McBeth, this Tour Series Z-Line plastic is a special, durable blend made particularly for signature discs.

The Z-Line Tour Series Luna is slightly more overstable than many other putters. It is a staple to have for playing both heavily wooded and open-style courses.
